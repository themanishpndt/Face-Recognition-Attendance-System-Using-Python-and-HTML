How to Use Smart Attendance

Important Rules

Name Requirements:
  • Use only alphabetic characters (A-Z, a-z) and spaces
  • Spaces are allowed between first name and last name
  • No numbers or special characters allowed
  • Example valid names: John Smith, Mary Jane Wilson
  • Example invalid names: John123, Tom@Work

Step 1: Register New User

  1. Click on "Capture Face" button on the home page
  2. Enter your name using only alphabets and spaces
  3. Look directly at the camera
  4. Stay still while your face is being captured
  5. Wait for the confirmation message

Step 2: Take Attendance

  1. Click on "Start Recognition" button
  2. Stand in front of the camera
  3. Ensure good lighting conditions
  4. Your attendance will be marked automatically
  5. Wait for the confirmation message

Step 3: View Records

  1. Click on "View Attendance" button
  2. View attendance records for the current date
  3. Records show name and timestamp of attendance
